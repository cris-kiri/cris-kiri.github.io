Identity, Values, and Strengths


Explain a situation where you have made an ethical decision. Discuss how you weighed up the values involved in that decision, the decision you made, and how you reflect on the decision now.

During a very rough work shift, I encountered an extremely rude customer that refused to listen about a policy that disallowed her from receiving a refund. After some abuse came my way and raised her voice, I was faced with dealing with the situation personally or elevating the problem to a manager. My limited encounters with handling such a situation may have jeopardized the professionalism of the business so I decided on the latter. The customer may not have got their way but it helped in maintaining the standards of the workplace and other customers notice these behaviors.

Describe how your culture and the people around you have influenced your values and identity.

I am a very “inward” person so I am not influenced by those around me and march to my own drums. I do look at those people more outside my circle like successful people around the world who have added value to the people that surround them and those who they influence around the world.

Identify your strengths and how they will support you during your learning journey.

I have an unquenchable love of learning so being part of the ever-evolving world of Web Development and partaking in such an exciting and innovative industry will aid in fostering that part of me.

Evaluate your limitations in terms of your learning and career development. How might these affect your learning journey?

I am very detail-oriented and this can be work for me but mostly against me. I tend to lose interest quickly if I don’t find something engaging and fun.

Share an example of when you were trying to work productively with others, but there was resistance or tension. Discuss strategies you tried at the time, how effective they were, and your reflections on what other strategies you would try now and why.

I can’t recall any major situations but small moments dealing with fellow staff members not pulling their weight or treating others poorly where I would either speak with their superiors or lead by example. Now I would speak with people about my concerns directly if it truly troubles me.
